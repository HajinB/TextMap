Problem

Imagine being blind and hungry walking around in a city. How would you get the information to know what to eat? Most restaurants do not provide braille versions of their menus - and the usual practice is to let someone read the menu for you. What if you could get the information directly on your phone or to your smart watch? One of the barriers faced by people in the real world is accessing information such as that would affect their lifes.

Survey

We ran a survey balbala which shows that some people face barriers when walking around in the world.
