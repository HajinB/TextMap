Problem

Imagine being blind and hungry walking around in a city. How would you get the information to know what to eat? Most restaurants do not provide braille versions of their menus - and the usual practice is to let someone read the menu for you. What if you could get the information directly on your phone or to your smart watch? One of the barriers faced by people in the real world is accessing information such as that would affect their lifes.

Solution

An app that can access information in the real world around you. Designed for everyone that wants to get information about a nearby object or a place directly on their phone.

Transform real world text into accessible text on your phone.
Users can scan signs, menus or any other object so that repeated scanning is not needed.
Find already scanned information about an object or place by searching
Recieve personalized notifications about the world around you decided by a filter
